About

Mr.Arun

Journalist

Full Name: Arun Bhusan
Profession: Former Chief Executive of the Bharat Times Group, founder-publisher, and chief editor of Bharat Times.
Honors: Awarded the esteemed Padma Bhushan award.
Mr. Arun is a retired journalist with over 25 years of experience in the business, and he is widely recognized. He covered many important stories and events over his successful career, garnering honors like the Bharatendu Harishchandra Awards, the Ramnath Goenka Excellence in Journalism Awards, and the Guillermo Cano World Press Freedom Prize.
Mr. Arun is committed to passing on his extensive knowledge, vision, advice, and expertise to the upcoming generation of journalists as a retired journalist.
According to Mr. Arun, journalism is about discovering truths that have the ability to inspire and ignite change, not just relaying facts. His mentorship programs and masterclasses are designed to develop a new generation of journalists who can write with impact and purpose.
